TYPES OF DOODLES

Zentangle

Zentangles are based on:
Focus - each stroke is created with intentNo Eraser - incorporate your mistakes into your design Foundations (or limits) from using 'strings' Abstract - move up or down, Ceremony - relax

Mandala

According to Hinduism and Buddhism, a mandala means "circle." Traditionally, a mandala is a geometric design. "It's about finding peace in the symmetry of the design and the universe," said artist Saudamini Madra.
